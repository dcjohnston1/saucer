## What Hana Does

Hana is an AI assistant that reads your Gmail inbox, identifies what actually
matters — tasks, appointments, bills, family logistics — and gives you a morning
briefing each day with the most important items. You can also ask her follow-up
questions in chat, and she can suggest tasks from your emails for you to accept
or dismiss with a swipe.

---

## Getting Set Up

1. Open the app: https://saucer-frontend-6ksi6iut7a-uc.a.run.app
2. Sign in with your Google account (emily.osteen.johnston@gmail.com)
3. When prompted, grant Hana permission to read your Gmail
   — she reads email but never sends anything without showing you a draft first
4. Wait a minute or two for Hana to process your inbox on first load

---

## Your First Few Days

**Morning briefing:** Each morning, look for a briefing card when you open the
app. It summarizes what Hana found that needs your attention today.

**Chat:** Tap "Let's chat" on the briefing card to ask Hana anything about what
she found. She'll have the full context.

**Suggested tasks:** Tap "Scan for tasks" on the email screen. Hana will suggest
action items from your emails. Swipe right to add one to your to-do list, swipe
left to dismiss it.

**Email highlights:** If you accept a suggested task, you can tap it to see the
exact text in the email that prompted the suggestion.
